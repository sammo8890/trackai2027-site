TrackAI2027

TrackAI2027 is an independent benchmark tracking project designed to monitor progress toward key predictions from the AI2027 forecast paper. This site aggregates capability, ecosystem, and timeline data to help clarify where we are—and where we might be headed—as AI accelerates.

This site is not affiliated with the authors of AI2027. It independently tracks progress against its public forecasts. For original forecasts, see: ai-2027.com

This website was built with the assistance of AI. Over 1.2 kWh of energy was used in model inference alone—roughly equivalent to powering a dishwasher cycle or running a 100W bulb for 12 hours.
